Rules:

  • The goal of the game is to get the most amount of points.
  • Breaking any brick awards you 1 point.
  • Whoever breaks all their bricks first is awarded a bonus.

Controls:

Computer

  • Left and right arrow keys or mouse move to move the paddle.
  • Space to launch ball.

Phone

  • Paddle follows finger.
  • Double tap to launch ball.
